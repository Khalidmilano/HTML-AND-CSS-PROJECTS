Easy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan.
  3. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown.

Simple Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200 grams pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta until tender.
  2. Drain the pasta.
  3. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  4. Mix the pasta with the sauce and serve.
